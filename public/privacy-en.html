Hidden Line Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026

한국어 개인정보처리방침

1. Overview

Hidden Line is a precision tracing game where players follow a hidden line with their finger. This privacy policy applies to the current Google Play test build.

2. Account and identity information

The current build does not require account creation, login, or social profile connection. It does not collect identity information such as name, email address, phone number, postal address, or user ID.

3. Game progress and settings

The current build may store game records, difficulty choices, language preference, and practice state locally on the user's device. This information is used to show daily records, weekly records, gameplay progress, and saved settings.

In the current build, this information is not transmitted to the developer's servers or to third parties. Deleting the app or clearing app data may remove these local records.

4. Network, backend, and AI

The project includes prepared Supabase backend and server-side AI integration boundaries for future features. However, the current Google Play test build does not enable account sync, server rankings, payment entitlement checks, or user-facing AI coaching.

If these features are enabled in a future release, this privacy policy and the Google Play Data Safety answers will be updated before release.

5. Ads and in-app purchases

The current build does not include advertising SDKs or in-app purchases. If ads, payments, or subscriptions are added, this policy will be updated to describe the related data handling.

6. Permissions and sensitive information

The current build does not request access to sensitive information such as location, contacts, photos, videos, microphone, calendar, health information, or financial information.

7. Third-party sharing

The current build does not sell or share personal information with third parties. If third-party SDKs or external services are added in the future, the related data handling will be disclosed in this policy and in the Google Play Data Safety section.

8. Children

Hidden Line is not primarily directed to children. If future store positioning or target age changes, the content rating and privacy disclosures will be reviewed again.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the developer through the support contact listed in the Google Play store listing.
