Hidden Line Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-26

Hidden Line is a precision tracing game. This policy describes the current Google Play build prepared from this repository.

Account and identity

Hidden Line does not currently require account creation, login, or a social profile to play.

Game progress and settings

The current build stores play records, difficulty choices, language preference, and local practice state on the device. These records are used to show daily progress and improve the play experience.

Network, backend, and AI

The app project has backend and AI integration boundaries prepared for future versions, but this current build does not enable account sync, server ranking, payment entitlement checks, or user-facing AI features. If those features are enabled later, this policy and the Google Play Data Safety answers must be updated before release.

Ads and purchases

The current build does not include advertising SDKs or in-app purchases.

Children

Hidden Line is not intentionally directed to children. If future store positioning changes, the content rating and privacy disclosures must be reviewed again.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the support contact listed in the Google Play store listing.
